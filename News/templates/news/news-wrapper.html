Nikan Hospital Group and Roam Engineering Development Group in pursuit of their social responsibility after the completion of the project to build a children's training center in the 20th District of Tehran (Ray Town), in collaboration with the Iliya Charity Foundation, are planning to help rebuild earthquake areas. Kermanshah Province will build 20 residential units and one educational unit in the areas and provide them with public access. The construction of these units will take place as soon as possible and subject to retrofitting standards.
